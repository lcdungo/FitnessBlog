ABOUT THE AUTHOR

The one lucky enough to experience all of these

Luz Therese Dungo is a student at UP Baguio who is currently taking a class in PE 2: Walking for Fitness, under Dr. Jocelyn Alberto-Floresca. She favors walking compared to hitching a ride when going around Baguio. Taking this class made her love walking even more because of the company she is with and the knowledge she acquires. She enrolled in this course with the expectation of just walking around the campus. Little did she know, all of her expectations are going to be exceeded the longer she spends time journeying to fitness through walking. If walking is what you love to do, e-mail her at lcdungo@up.edu.ph.
