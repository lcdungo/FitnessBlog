WALKING FOR FITNESS

“introduces students to the theory knowledge and practical experience in the principles of walking as it relates to fitness.”

It aims to:

  • provide students with walking activities that would improve their health;
  • instill a healthy fitness mindset that would aid students to choose wisely in living towards a healthy lifestyle;
  • instill an appreciation of walking as an exercise;
  • and improve a student’s overall fitness through active participation during class.

Reference: PE 2 - Walking for Fitness Syllabus by Dr. Jocelyn Alberto-Floresca
